How IMD-We Works

Register for a new account and set up a profile. Once logged in, users can search for titles using the IMDB API, and save their favorite movies to their profiles. Users can then see what their friends have been enjoying lately by viewing their profile, or see a full list of favorited movies on their personal dashboard.

Created by Mike Godwin, Erik Roberts, Jason Dehaemers, and Jonathan Garro
